Simple print utility for Chess FEN notation

## Article

A utility to parse and pretty-print FEN (Forsyth-Edwards Notation) chess positions. It's useful for chess programmers, but it's a one-file OCaml program that does exactly one thing. Competent implementation, but very narrow scope.
